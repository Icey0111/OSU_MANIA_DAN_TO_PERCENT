# Privacy Notice

osu!mania Dan Voting stores only the information needed to authenticate players and operate community voting:

- osu! user ID, username, and avatar URL returned by osu! OAuth;
- submitted Dan rank and sub-rank votes;
- official beatmap identifiers or the MD5 checksum and basic metadata of a local beatmap;
- short-lived login handoff records for the locally installed overlay; and
- administrator audit records for local-to-official beatmap promotions.

The project does not upload `.osu` files, audio, background images, local file paths, osu! passwords, or osu! OAuth access tokens. Login handoff records expire after ten minutes and are consumed after use.

Public voting results are aggregated. Individual voter information is available only to authorized administrators for moderation and export. To request correction or deletion of your stored account and votes, contact the project owner through the repository issue tracker.

This is an independent community project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by osu! or ppy Pty Ltd.
