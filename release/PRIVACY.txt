osu!mania Dan Voting - Privacy Summary
======================================

This independent community project stores your osu! user ID, username, avatar
URL, and submitted votes. For local beatmaps it stores an MD5 checksum and
basic beatmap metadata.

It does not upload .osu files, audio, backgrounds, local file paths, osu!
passwords, or osu! OAuth access tokens. Individual voter details are available
only to authorized administrators for moderation and export.
